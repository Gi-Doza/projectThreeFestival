OPEN DOORS festival

Dates: December 6-8, 2024

Open Doors Festival is a three-day community event dedicated to raising awareness and support for homelessness and housing solutions. Through engaging panels, workshops, art exhibits, and a benefit concert, the festival brings together experts, advocates, and the community to explore actionable solutions, celebrate resilience, and foster hope for a brighter future.
