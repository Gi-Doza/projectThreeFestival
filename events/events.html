06

Friday

2:00 PM

Opening Event: Pathways to Home”

The festival kicks off with a panel featuring leaders from Bay Area nonprofits, local government officials, and housing advocates. They’ll discuss current homelessness challenges, affordable housing solutions, and ways the community can get involved. This event will include a Q&A session where attendees can ask questions and learn about specific ways they can help.

December

07

Saturday

10:00 AM

Morning Workshop: Building Hope Kits

Attendees work together to assemble “Hope Kits” for individuals currently experiencing homelessness. Kits may include hygiene items, non-perishable snacks, socks, gloves, and information on local resources. Volunteers can also write notes of encouragement to include in each kit.

1:00 PM

Afternoon Workshop: Stories from the Streets

A moderated session where individuals who have experienced homelessness share their stories, discuss challenges, and offer insights on what helped them. This powerful event humanizes the issue and encourages empathy and understanding.

6:00 PM

Evening Panel: Affordable Housing Solutions

This panel will bring together architects, city planners, and housing experts to discuss innovative solutions for affordable housing, from tiny homes and modular units to policy changes. Attendees will learn about potential solutions and how they can advocate for change in their own communities.

December

08

Sunday

2:00 PM

Afternoon Art Exhibit: Stories Through Art

A curated exhibit showcasing artwork created by individuals who have experienced homelessness, alongside pieces inspired by the housing crisis. The exhibit highlights the resilience and creativity of those affected while providing a reflective and emotional experience for attendees.

5:00 PM

Evening Concert: Music for Hope

A benefit concert featuring local musicians and artists who support the cause. The concert will serve as a celebratory and unifying event to close the festival, bringing the community together in solidarity. Proceeds from ticket sales will support local homeless shelters and services.
